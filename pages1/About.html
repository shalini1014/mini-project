About Us


The website aims to provide an interactive platform to interact with those students who have faced the company interview and can chat with the senior person according to their interest as one feels hesitation in a new atmosphere and as a output they are unable to interact with teachers or seniors about certain things. In addition, they will also get some more information which will help them to face interview with confidence and to overcome certain problems that are faced by a fresher as experience plays a major role in all field. So, we named it “GLA CONNECTIFY” which suggests to connect GLA students and management together on a single platform.
